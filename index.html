Our Products

Premium Manufactured Sand

Premium Manufactured Sand

At RS Blue Metals, we produce 4-Stage M-Sand using the latest manufacturing techniques to ensure superior quality and consistency. Our advanced processes guarantee that both M-Sand and P-Sand meet the highest standards required for modern construction.
M-Sand offers a higher Fineness Modulus Index compared to natural river sand, delivering excellent workability for concrete applications. With a granulometric composition ranging from 150 microns to 3.5 mm, our M-Sand is ideally suited for a wide range of construction processes.
The perfect grading and rounded particle shape of our M-Sand provide enhanced strength, durability, and longevity to concrete, making it a reliable choice for sustainable construction.

4 Stage P-Sand

Our Plaster Sand is suitable for both External and Internal plastering.
Granulometric composition of P-Sand ranges between 150 microns to 2.25mm, ideal for block masonry and plastering purposes.
P-Sand is free from silt and clay particles, offering better abrasion resistance, higher unit weight, and lower permeability.

Aggregates

Our team is committed to maintaining proper gradation, shape, and quality of aggregates from our manufacturing process.
Products offered by RS Blue Metals are 40mm, 20mm, 12mm, 6mm, Wet mix, GSB, and Dust.

Director’s Message

The continuous growth of the global construction sector has elevated mining to a position of great importance, with Aggregates, M-Sand, and P-Sand emerging as vital contributors to infrastructure development. With natural river sand becoming increasingly scarce and unsustainable, the demand for reliable alternatives has never been greater.

As construction expands across domestic, commercial, industrial, and public sectors, the need for high-quality substitutes has driven the emergence of M-Sand and P-Sand. Although river sand imports exist, they fall far short of meeting the immense market demand. This created the necessity for locally produced, sustainable solutions.

At this juncture, RS Blue Metals ventured into the production of Aggregates, M-Sand, and P-Sand, addressing both social responsibility and industrial needs. With deep knowledge of the industry and its challenges, we have steadily advanced towards our goal of becoming one of the nation’s most trusted and leading players in the sector.

Our planned infrastructure expansion and new production facilities will significantly enhance our capacity. With the expertise and dedication of our skilled team, we are well-positioned to meet the ever-growing demands of the construction industry.

With your continued trust and support, RS Blue Metals is confident of scaling new heights and contributing to stronger, sustainable growth in the construction sector.
